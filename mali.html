About Mali

License: Open Font License

Mali is a Thai and Latin family which was inspired by a 6th graders' handwriting. It exudes a carefree and naive appearance. Cadson Demak is the first Thai communication design firm to develop type design solutions. Founded in 2002, the studio came together through a shared love of typography and design, a wish to expand and modernize the font industry as a whole, and the desire to make everyday use of type more accessible.
